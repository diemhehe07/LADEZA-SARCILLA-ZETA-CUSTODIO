Our Mission at SLSU

SLSU-U Matter is dedicated to supporting the mental wellness of SLSU Lucena students. We provide accessible counseling, resources, and peer support tailored to the unique challenges faced by college students.

Through education and empathy, we aim to normalize seeking help and empower students to prioritize their mental health alongside their academic goals.

Our Vision for SLSU Students

To create a campus environment where every SLSUan feels supported, understood, and empowered to achieve both academic success and personal well-being.
