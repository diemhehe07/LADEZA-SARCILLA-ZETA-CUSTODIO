About SLSU-U Matter

Learn about our mission to support the mental well-being of SLSU Lucena students and our dedicated campus team

Our Story at SLSU Lucena

SLSU-U Matter was established in 2022 as a dedicated mental health initiative for Southern Luzon State University Lucena students. Recognizing the unique pressures faced by college students, we created a campus-based support system tailored to the SLSU community.

Our Mission for SLSU Students

We aim to promote mental wellness awareness and provide accessible support tools specifically designed for SLSU Lucena students. Our goal is to make mental health resources readily available throughout your academic journey.

Through campus-wide education and peer support, we work to normalize seeking help and empower students to prioritize their emotional well-being alongside their academic success.

Our Vision for SLSU Community

To create a campus environment where every SLSUan feels supported, understood, and empowered to achieve both academic excellence and personal growth. We envision SLSU Lucena as a model for student mental health support in the region.

Our Values

Student-Centered

We prioritize the unique needs and experiences of SLSU students in all our programs and services.

Campus Confidentiality

Your privacy is protected within campus guidelines. All counseling sessions remain strictly confidential.

Inclusive Support

We serve all SLSU students regardless of course, year level, background, or personal circumstances.

Academic Integration

Our approaches complement your academic journey and support your overall educational experience.

Campus Partnerships

SLSU-U Matter collaborates with various campus departments to provide comprehensive support:

  • Office of Student Affairs: Coordinating student activities and wellness programs
  • Academic Departments: Supporting students with course-related stress
  • University Health Services: Integrating mental and physical health care
  • Student Organizations: Promoting mental health awareness across campus
